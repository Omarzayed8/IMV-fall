Process

To start my adventure, I knew I had to capture the essence of these two incredible cities. With the help of Figma, I sketched the iconic skylines of Toronto and Montreal, each defined by their unique landmarks and towering buildings. My storybook wouldn't be complete without a nod to the major train stops that connected these two metropolises. I meticulously recreated the grandeur of Union Station in Toronto and Gare Central in Montreal, ensuring every architectural detail was brought to life. Adding a touch of visual magic, I scattered stars across the pages and created majestic towers using simple shapes.
